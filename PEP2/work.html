Week 12

I conducted interviews with four of our classmates and asked them a set of questions that we transcripted so we can do our case study and find out which filters would be necessary on the website, which details on available accommodation should ideally be displayed, etc. We had our set of questions already written in a document that I would read out loud from, in order to avoid any human error of forgetting to ask something, or formulating the question differently for the interviewees which would impact their answer.

Each of us has written down the transcript of an interview. Most of those questioned said pictures are important, the full price being presented, and a map function so that they could see where the accommodation's location is on a map and compare it to the university's and Fontys campus' locations.

This is a screenshot from our Survey result document. We got rid of spam/ irrelevant sruvey answers and gathered and filtered together the answers to our questions that would help us.
Through our survey, we analyzed the way other international students have managed to find student accommodation so far, which websites/ platforms they used, how long searching for something took them before finding a room, their accommodation situation (shared - between how many people, which facilities are shared; indivdual), and how they keep in contact with their landlord.
We could look at the model of our competitors, the housing agencies that are already out there, to see what we should implement and what we should add to make our platform better.

Following the presentation we were given, we created user personas (with pictures of people that do not exist and are only AI generated) that encapsulate the results of our surveys and interviews, so we can use our data more easily.

We used these personas to come up with a document where we state which features to implement on our platform: Log in system (only if the user is applying for the place), chat or email, logo, embedded map, review section, filters section.
