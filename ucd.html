Our team came up with a vision statement document where we describe the goals and objects of the artist website, define the target audience, identify the unique product proposition, user experience, and craft the statement.


Our statement is: “Our goal is to create an artist website that showcases the artist's unique style and creative process, while also providing a platform for art enthusiasts and potential fans to connect with the artist.

About UCD

We use User Centered Design to cater for a user who keeps evolving and to provide something useful and easy to use for them, based on psychology and anthropological studies.

Usability makes things go faster and minimzes processing expenses, as well as collects data faster in relation to how often a website is used, generating revenue.

On the right you can see my answer to the question "How do you see yourself as a UX designer in the future?"

The role of a UX designer always changes and improves.

In the virtual world it's important to use a language that meets the user's requirements in language and accesibility, while in the ohysical world it's not so easy switching from business to business or shop to shop and you are inclined to prefer to ask someone for more information instead of "skipping". Of course, UCD laws can be broken in our advantage.
