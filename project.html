About Project Erion

This semester we met Erion, a Greek artist and engineer who our teams of students will work for.

Our goal is to create a website that displays his artwork for people interested in seeing it, buying it, as well as contacting Erion for inquiries and custom artpieces.

In order to achieve this, our team works together to combine Erion's wishes with what would be best for his website according to user research, using what the subjects we have teach us: Media Production, User-Centered Design, and Developement classes.

About our team: Tableau de l'est

 

Our group for the project is called “Tableau de l’est”, which is French for “Painting of the East”, as all the members are originally from countries that are East from the Netherlands, and because the project is centered about helping a Greek artist and engineer, Erion, display his artworks.

We are motivated to manage to make Erion’s wishes come true when it comes to a website where he can show and sell his art, as well as let people interested in his work get in touch with him for custom pieces or purchases.

You can see pictures of each team member on the right.

 

 

Progress and what I did

Using DOT framework and aiming to prioritize the consumer needs, our team started by gathering questions for an interview with Erion, then managed to come up with a survey and an interview to be conducted for potential users, whose results will be combined with the stakeholder's empathy map in order to achieve and facilitate a result that is up to expectations.

We regularly conduct team meetings to be on board with what we have to do and manage to use each of the team member's best abilities to work together for a satisfying results.

To help my team make progress on the project, I helped come up with interview questions for our stakeholder, survey and interview questions for users, and helped distribute the questions. I attend group meetings and contribute to teamwork.
